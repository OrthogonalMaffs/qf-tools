When you interact with a dApp, you often grant it permission to spend your tokens. If that contract is compromised, your tokens are at risk. Review and revoke approvals you no longer need.

  • See which contracts can spend your tokens
  • Revoke individual approvals with one click
  • Approval history and risk indicators

Service fee: A small QF fee applies per revoke transaction. A portion is burned, the remainder supports QF Tools development. Fee is adjustable as QF value changes.
